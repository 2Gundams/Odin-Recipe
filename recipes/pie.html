Apple Pie Filling

Ingredients:

  • Six Medium Apples, Thinly Sliced
  • Three Quarter Cup Sugar
  • Two tbsp Flour
  • Three Quater tsp Cinnamon
  • One Quarter tsp Salt
  • One Eighth tsp Nutmeg
  • One tbsp Lemon Juice

Instructions:

  1. Peel apples then slice apples thinly
  2. Mix all ingredients into a bowl
